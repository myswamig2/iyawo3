Module 3— Abata "The Swamp"

The Abata in this case is an attachment uniquely afixed to extend the five(5) possibilities of the Four Cowrie Divination System into Nine(9) scenarios of the Obi Abata Divination As Iyawo many things are still murky and unclear. These attachments transform Four Cowrie Shells into Obi Orisha and are benefecial to Iyawos and understanding how to create something from nearly nothing and reveal completely new perspective.

The nine scenarios of the Obi Abata are applied directly to Obi Orisha. This is a direct application of 'Standing on the Shoulders of Our Ancestors'. In this module, casting Obi Orisha and identifying the associated orisha is explored.
